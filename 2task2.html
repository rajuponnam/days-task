Dulquer Salmaan

Dulquer Salmaan an accomplished Indian actor and film producer predominantly works in the Malayalam cinema Born on July 28, 1986, in Kochi, Kerala, he is the son of veteran actor Mammootty. Dulquer made his acting debut in 2012 with the film "Second Show" and quickly rose to fame with his versatile performancesSalmaan Known for his charming screen presence and acting prowess, he has starred in a variety of genres, including romantic dramas, thrillers, and comedies Some of his notable films include "Bangalore Days," "Charlie," "Kurup," and "Ustad Hotel." Apart from Malayalam, he has also acted in Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi films, establishing a pan-Indian appeal. Dulquer's contributions to cinema have earned him several awards and a massive fan following across the country.
